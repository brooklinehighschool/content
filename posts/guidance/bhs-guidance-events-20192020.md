		9/12/2019
	

**September 16, Monday T block – Mandatory Student Senior Assembly –** Steps to Post High School Planning **-** Auditorium   
  
​**September 17, Tuesday – 6:30pm – Combined PTO Senior Parent Night – Overview of Post High School Planning with College Focus** by Lenny Libenzon, BHS College Counselor and Darby Neff-Verre- Auditorium  
  
**September 28, Saturday – 8:30am-12:30 pm – PTO College Essay Workshop –** BHS  
  
**October 3rd, Thursday - Back to School Night**  
  
**October 16, Wednesday – 7 pm – BHS Guidance sponsored Massachusetts Educational Financing Authority College Financial Aid Seminar for students/caretakers–** Auditorium. Junior students and families are invited to attend too!  
  
**October 19, Saturday – _PSAT at BHS_ – starts 7:45 REGISTRATION FOR JUNIORS IS ONLINE.**  Sophomores may register but note that this test is normed for juniors in high school.  
  
​**December 11, Tuesday – 6:30 pm – Junior and Sophomore Students and Caretakers:** Guidance overview of the Jr./Sr. Timelines and Summit College Admissions Testing (SAT and ACT) presentation **–** Auditorium. Sophomores are invited to attend too!  
  
**Mid to late December –** PSAT scores available online to students   
  
**January 6, Monday - Junior Assembly**\- Starting Post High School Planning **–** Auditorium  
  
**January 15, Wednesday –** **8-9:15 am –** Tentative Sophomore PTO/ BHS Guidance-Counseling Breakfast – Next Steps to Junior Year and Beyond – Location TBD  
  
**January 13, Monday – 6:30 - 8:00 pm - Gap Year Fair at BHS -** MLK and Café for senior students and parents-caretakers. Juniors students and families are invited to attend too!  
  
**February 12, Tuesday, 6:30 pm - Junior Parent and Student Night –** Auditorium  
  
**March 19, 6:00 - 8:00 pm - BHS College Fair** for juniors and their families- Café and Overflow area  
  
**April 1, Wednesday – 6:30 -8:30 pm – BHS Guidance and Special Education Depts sponsor –** Support Services and College for seniors and caretakers **–** MLK. Juniors and caretakers are invited too!  
  
**April 13, Monday – 6:30 pm –** BHS Guidance sponsored **MEFA Paying for College Seminar** for seniors and caretakers  
  
**April 29, Wednesday - BHS Career Fair**
