​**JUNIORS AND SENIORS – Students and Parents-Caregivers**  
**THIS WEDNESDAY….** **October 17 – 7 pm – BHS Guidance sponsored MA Educational Financing Authority (MEFA) College Financial Aid Seminar  - Auditorium**  
Junior and Senior parents and students are invited to attend a free seminar on paying for college. This seminar is offered through MEFA, the Commonwealth’s authority on planning, saving, and paying for college. An experienced MEFA representative will provide an overview of the college financing process, including types of financial aid, how to apply, and financing options for your family. Before the seminar, be sure to visit MEFA’s website — [mefa.org](https://protect-us.mimecast.com/s/e4pcCqxMZ5u832mJfZgmDT?domain=mefa.org) — to download a copy of the seminar presentation in English or Spanish and explore other tools and resources to assist you in the college planning process.  
  
**SENIORS**  
**All senior seminars** are now over. Any senior who has not yet attended 2 seminars and/or met with their counselor should contact their counselor this week to talk next steps.

The Improbable Players- **Improbable Players uses _theater performances_ to address addiction, alcoholism, and the opioid epidemic. Our performances include a talkback/Q&A and give students the space and tools to combat social pressures and find coping strategies that work for them. Plays are based on true stories and performed by people in recovery**
