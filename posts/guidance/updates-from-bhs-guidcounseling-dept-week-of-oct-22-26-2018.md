**ALL STUDENTS**  
Coffee talk Thurs Oct 25 – X block:  
Come hear from Signal Engineers from Fischbach and Moore Electrical Group about their career paths.   
Did you know? Signal Engineers in MA on average make $82,000 per year? In a rail environment, signaling engineering enables operators to regulate the movement of trains on the network with minimum conflict or delay. A signaling engineer can choose to specialize in design, construction, and maintenance or project management.
