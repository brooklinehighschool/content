Happy New Year!

1/4/2021

0 Comments

 
This blog post is brought to you by:  Zach Gale, Sophomore, Member of Student Council

Leading up to winter break, Student Council continued to analyze the results of the surveys you filled out. We are using the data from the surveys to write bills to address your concerns and interests. Student Council also met with Faculty Council to discuss several pending bills including: the Sick Absence Bill, Right to Rest Bill, and Test Grade Transparency Bill.  Other pending bills include the Student Council and Faculty Council Meeting Amendment, Grade Rounding Bill, and the Judiciary Reform Bill. If you have any questions, concerns, or ideas, please do not hesitate to contact one of your BHS Student Representatives here.
