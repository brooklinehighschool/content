Looking for something to do during your down time? Want to explore new careers and what it takes to get there? Check out **Naviance**, a website used to not only apply to college but also explore your career interests and much more! Check it out at [https://student.naviance.com/auth/fclookup  (Links to an external site.)](https://student.naviance.com/auth/fclookup)  
Need help signing in? Feel free to email Ms. Kennedy at [kate\_kennedy@psbma.org](mailto:kate_kennedy@psbma.org) for help!
