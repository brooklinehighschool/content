Juniors:  
​  
 -- SAT now offering August 26 test date at Brookline High (BHS deadline for SSD requests for 8/26 SAT will be June 16)  
\-- ACT Sept 9 test date - reg deadline/ SSD request deadline August 4 (BHS deadline for SSD requests for 9/9 ACT will be June 16)  
  
  
Seniors:  
As you know, we rely on Naviance data to help BHS juniors and seniors through the college process.  In order to get accurate information, I am asking you to log in to your Naviance account and enter the result for each college you’ve applied to (Admit, Deny, Waitlist, Incomplete, etc). Please click on the “Colleges I am Applying To,” and then on the pencil / edit option.   
In addition, we need to send your final transcript to the school you plan to attend.  Please fill out the yellow “Request for Final Grades” form that you can get from your counselor or in our office, room 162 by Friday May 19.  

  

Comments are closed.
